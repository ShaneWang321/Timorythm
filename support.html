Frequently Asked Questions

How do I customize my day's start and end times?

Go to Settings and adjust the "Day Start Time" and "Day End Time" options to match your schedule.

How do I create a new task?

Navigate to the Task List tab and tap the "+" button in the top right corner. Fill in the task details and tap "Save".

How do I change the app's language?

Go to Settings and select your preferred language from the Language options.

Can I customize the visual theme?

Yes, go to Settings and select your preferred theme from the Theme options.

How do task reminders work?

When creating or editing a task, you can set a reminder time. The app will send you a notification at the specified time.

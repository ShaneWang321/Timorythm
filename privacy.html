Privacy Policy

Introduction

This Privacy Policy outlines how 時序 (Timorythm) collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our mobile application.

We are committed to ensuring that your privacy is protected. Any information you provide will only be used in accordance with this privacy statement.

This policy is effective as of January 1, 2023.

Information We Collect

時序 (Timorythm) is designed with your privacy in mind. All data created within the app (including tasks, settings, and preferences) is stored locally on your device.

  • Task information you create within the app
  • App preferences and settings
  • Usage data to improve app performance

We do not collect or transmit personal information to external servers.

How We Use Your Information

The information stored locally on your device is used solely for the purpose of providing app functionality, including:

  • Displaying your tasks and time management information
  • Sending local notifications for task reminders
  • Customizing app appearance based on your preferences

Sharing of Information

We do not share or transmit your data to third parties.

Your data remains on your device unless you explicitly choose to export it or back it up using your device's backup services (such as iCloud).

Data Security

We are committed to ensuring that your information is secure. The app uses standard iOS security mechanisms to protect your data.

All app data is stored in the secure app sandbox provided by iOS.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the app.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the support page.
